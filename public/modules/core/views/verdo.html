Introduction

This project covered the redesign concept development and protoyping of Verdo's web ecosystem. Among hundreds of pages and a mess of contact information, Verdo was built on an information architecture that can't handle the scale of it's content any longer. The redesign initiative is focusing on expanding the entire ecosystem to mobile devices, which were not even considered to this point.
