About me A few words describing the person behind the selected projects

Dan Mindru Aarhus, Denmark

3 years ago I wrote a description of myself that I thought shows how I am as a professional. To this date, no matter how hard I try, I still can't make a better one. Here goes:

Feeling

I consider myself to be a combination between a perfectionist, analyst and organizer. I often give too much attention to details. This results in overwork, but I do like a challenge. Challenges are based on problems and problems are always where I begin. As a designer I believe that the foundation of every project is a well defined problem. As a programmer I know every problem can be solved and my aim is to do it in an efficient way. There is no innovation if it doesn’t solve a problem, no creativity if it doesn’t find an original solution to do so.

Purpose

It often happens that I just know my ideas are right. Think about it. It happens to all of us. If you have to take a decision that will influence your business or career, you just feel it is the right one. This feeling will come sooner or later, you just have to listen to it.

Love

I very rarely find design that has a mind-boggling grab of my visual senses. We all fall in love. Some are able to just fall in love with people, some with objects, and some with both. I fall in love with design.
